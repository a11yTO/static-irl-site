Public Health Guidelines

Attendees will be required to follow public health guidelines set out by the province and the venue. Currently, our venue requires proof of vaccination or a negative test. Still, we cannot guarantee this will be in effect this fall, as it is not a general requirement in Ontario.

If masks are not mandatory in October, we encourage you to still wear one whenever you wish, and we will have some available at the venue.
