The Event

#a11yIRL, or Accessibility in Real Life, focuses on the physical experience of accessibility. We will provide you with an opportunity to learn about:

  • Universal design and inclusive design;
  • Inclusion and representation; and
  • Designing for people with disabilities

Join us on Thursday, October 21st at 6pm EST for an evening of Accessibility In Real Life.
